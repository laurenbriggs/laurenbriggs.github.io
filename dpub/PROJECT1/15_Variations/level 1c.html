THE
      PESTO
      MANIFESTO


            Peter
            Nowogrodzki


                  This
                  is
                  the
                  pesto
                  manifesto;


            an
            improvised
            recipe
            of
            sorts.


      When
      making
      pesto,


            Where
            are
            some
            things
            to
            keep
            in
            mind:


                  MUST
                  use
                  organic
                  garlic
                  if
                  you
                  live
                  in
                  the
                  21st
                  Ce.


            MUST
            use
            fresh
            basil
            from
            your
            mom
            or
            neighbor’s
            garden.


      MUST
      use
      pine
      nuts.


            NO
            food
            processing
            allowed.


                  These
                  are
                  important
                  because
                  pesto
                  is
                  a
                  delicacy
                  that
                  deserves
                  to
                  be
                  made
                  right.


            Pasta
            should
            not
            be
            smothered
            in
            mediocrity!
            Or
            should
            it?


      BECOME
      inspired
      by
      mediocre
      productions.


            DON'T
            covert
            production
            and
            experimentation
            recipe.


                  ALWAYS
                  leave
                  the
                  edges
                  rough,
                  so
                  that
                  someone
                  can
                  cut
                  themselves.


            A
            photoshop’d
            joke
            is
            deep
            and
            meaningful.


      Take
      the
      knife
      to
      cultural
      icons
      and
      produce:


            produce
            the
            produce
            for
            the
            recipe
            and
            destroy
            the
            recipe.


                  Pun
                  and
                  play,
                  annihilate
                  and
                  resurrect
                  meaning.


            Keep
            in
            mind:
            pesto
            is
            delicious
            when
            it’s
            made
            fresh.
