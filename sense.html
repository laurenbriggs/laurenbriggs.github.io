SENSE
OF PLACE
Sense of Place is a poster series that is emblematic of two cities of Morocco: Chefchaouen and Fes. Mosaics and colourful tiles are an inherent theme throughout all of Morocco and thus are imperative when transposing the aesthetic of two of its greatest cities to a printed poster. The mosaic is printed onto a heavy white A2 stock, with the silhouette laser cut from a heavy black A2 stock, the pages can then be displayed superimposed or alongside one another.
